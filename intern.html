Internship Opportunities

SAIBU EWADE LEGAL CONSULT is Recruiting Volunteers for the post of a “legal blog writer”.

Perks

- Exposure to a diverse range of legal topics - Networking opportunities within the legal community - Professional development and skill enhancement - Collaboration with legal Experts - Data Allowance for writers

Key Responsibilities:

- Crafting engaging and informative legal blog posts - Conducting research on relevant legal topics - Collaborating with our team to ensure content accuracy and quality - Contributing fresh perspectives to our growing legal community

BENEFITS OF THE INTERNSHIP

Great learning avenue, free trainings and mentorship as well as brain stimulation and challenging assignments.There is also an opportunity to become a part of a bright and vibrant team

INTERNSHIP STRUCTURE

Interns will undergo trainings and will be given tasks to complete within specified deadline. The internship program will provide an opportunity to experience a combination of research work and public speaking. There will be meetings scheduled to review activities and progress of interns before the end of their program.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

To be eligible to apply, you must be an undergraduate of a recognized university. Passionate applicants who fall outside this category may be considered.

SELECTION CRITERIA

Selections are made after thorough consideration of a candidate’s social and academic background including the candidate’s academic standing, leadership experience, past experience and extracurricular activities.

SELECTION PROCESS

• Applicants are required to send their CVs/resumes and an expression of interest (indicating the proposed duration of the internship) to saibuewadelegalconsult@gmail.com. Requirement of CV/Resume may be waived for students with limited experience but are passionate about the internship, upon indication.

• Successful applicants will be contacted within 3 working days of receipt of application.

• Due to the high volume of applications we receive at SELC, offers will remain open for acceptance for 14 working days from the date of the offer. Failure to communicate an acceptance of the offer will be deemed to be a rejection of the said offer and will consequently lead to a withdrawal of the offer. The next successful applicant will be offered the internship placement in such circumstance.

DURATION

Our internship programs usually run for a period between one – three months however, longer periods may be considered. The internship opportunities are offered all year round and will be on a rolling basis.
