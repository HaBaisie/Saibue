YUSUF JAMIU

Position: EXECUTIVE RESEARCH DIRECTOR

Yusuf Jamiu serves as the Executive Research Director at Saibu Ewade Legal Consult, where he plays a pivotal role in shaping the firm's research initiatives and legal strategies. With a deep passion for legal scholarship, he leads a dedicated team in conducting comprehensive research, staying at the forefront of legal developments, and ensuring the firm's clients receive cutting-edge insights and solutions.

Jamiu is a student at the Faculty of law, Ahmadu Bello University in Nigeria. He has keen interest in litigation practice and a penchant for intellectual property law, tax law, among others areas of law. In a bid to acclimatise himself with his interest in the legal practice, he has interned with various law firms both virtually and physically. Few of these law firms include: Platinum and Taylor Hill Legal Practice, Harlem Solicitor, B.L Ijaiya & Associates. Also, he has undertaken numerous online courses and programmes to widen his horizon on the areas of law that pique his interest. Recently, he concluded a 9 weeks mentorship session at Juliana Imam Intellectual Property Consult (JIIPC) to augment his knowledge in Intellectual property law. Aside from pursuing his interest in the legal profession, Jamiu is also an astute and outstanding student in the four walls of his classroom. He ranks among the top 2% in a class of 450 students. Notable is the fact his academic excellence has in no way deterred him from partaking in extracurricular activities. He is a member of ABU tax club, erstwhile research assistant at National ADR society, Assistant director of Research at Realist Chamber Ahmadu Bello University, among others and he has diligently and religously served in all the positions he occupy. With the National ADR Society, he contributed to the publication of different research papers. Also, in his role at Realist Chamber, ABU Zaria, he oversees and ensures preparation for and organisation of moot competitions. Jamiu is an ardent mooter, a researcher per excellence and a prolific writer. These qualities have earned him numerous achievements in recent time. Some of which include: Best Oralist and First Runner up at the just concluded Firdaous Integrated Services IHL moot competition, Winner and Best advocate at the LAWSAN UCC Ministry of Justice moot competition, Winner - Realist Intra-chamber Moot Competition, 1st Runner Up – Student advocate associatin, Lagos State University National Brief Writing Competition. In addition to these achievements, Jamiu has a desirable number of publications to his name. With his most recent publication titled “An analysis of service of court process in Nigeria vis-a-vis legal effect of defective service” featured in LAWSAN UNIUYO Journal (2022).
